My Childhood

I had a fun neighborhood growing up. Tons of kids pretty much the same age who all got together all day, every day. We had "shows" in the little log cabin playhouse in my back yard, swung on the long rope swing in Richard's back yard, climbed to impossible heights up the pine tree in Heidi's front yard, and made more than a little mischief!
